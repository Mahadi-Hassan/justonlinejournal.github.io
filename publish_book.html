Information for book authors & editors

Explore our book authors and editors pages to learn more about publishing a book with Springer. If you have an idea or proposal for a book, please get in contact with a publishing editor in your subject area. They can provide feedback and advice on how to move forward. The manuscript guideline pages also provide detailed instructions on how to prepare your manuscript in the best possible way. Ask your editor about open access publishing options.
